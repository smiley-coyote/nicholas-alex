    My Work

    • My Critic Review


      As a person who loves to go out to the movies but doesn't want to waste the money or time on something not completely satisfactory, I heavily rely on critic reviews. Not all critic reviews are trustworthy however, and it is sometimes difficult to find a somebody with the same movie interests as yourself. Imagine a website that matched you up with critics based on your previous rating history? This is the idea I had when building My Critic Review. I created an alogorithm that matches a user up with other users who have rated movies similarly. Follow your top matched critics, get a percentage of approval for all movies based on your top matches, and rate/review movies yourself for others to read. It's like Facebook for movie critics. Primary languages used were JavaScript, Node, React, and MongoDB.
